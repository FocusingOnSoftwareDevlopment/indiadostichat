Frequently Asked Questions

Can I play games with IndiaDostiChat users?

Yes, users can play games by opening the official game website, creating a room, and sharing the invite link inside IndiaDostiChat.

Does IndiaDostiChat host Skribbl, Chess, or Smash Karts directly?

IndiaDostiChat does not own Skribbl, Chess.com, Lichess, or Smash Karts. It only helps users discover and join games together.

How do I invite chat users to a game?

Simply create a private room on the game's website, copy the invite link, and paste it in the main chat room or send it directly to a friend.

Are the games free to play?

Yes, all the featured games on our platform (Skribbl, Lichess, Smash Karts) are completely free to play.

Can I share game room links in chat?

Yes! We encourage users to share invite links to play browser games with the community.

What rules should I follow while playing games?

Users should follow chat rules and avoid spam, abuse, or unsafe links. Always be respectful to other players.
